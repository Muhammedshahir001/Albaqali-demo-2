About Albaqali

Experienced &
Quality More Than
30 Years

Established in 1992 in Doha, Qatar, Al Baqali Automatic Doors is a leading provider of automatic systems for industrial and residential gates and roll-up shutters. With over 30 years of experience, the company continually innovates to meet market demands and is renowned for its excellent after-sale service, making it a pioneer in Qatar's automatic solutions industry.
